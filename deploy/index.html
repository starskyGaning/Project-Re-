Mintohardjo Naval Hospital

Providing quality health support and integrated health services to the public by prioritizing patients safety

Providing for the people since 1957

Mintohardjo Naval Hospital provides a wide range of surgical, medical & other services. the original hospital which was Djakarta Naval Hospital change its name into Mintohardjo Naval Hospital at 1974

After the name changes in 1974, Mintohardjo Naval Hospital keep extending its range of services, we work closely with communities that surrounds us in order to educate them about general health, etc.
